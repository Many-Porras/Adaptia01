Receive requirement
and / or project.

The client enters the requirement, incident and / or project in the tools of management, in which you can define the priority and the impact they have on your deal. Everything entered is automatically sent to the factory leader and is attended in the agreed times according to the SLA's assumed. The SLA is an agreement between the customer and provider that describe the type and responsibilities of the service for ensure the quality and standard of deliverables.

Under this scheme, Altimea undertakes to respond quickly and effectively as requested for the client. In addition, to have a continuous and fluid communication about the state of the requested requirement at all times.

Analyze
the case.

After the case is entered, the project leader transfers the requirement to the UX, design, functional and technical analysts. The analysis is carried out in all the areas that concern the development of the product to propose the best solution. At this stage it is necessary to clearly understand what the client is asking for, if necessary, Altimea will meet with the key stakeholders to clarify doubts and clearly understand what is desired.

The output of this stage is a set of documents such as the requirements specification functional, quality requirements specification, meeting minutes, among others. These Documents will be shared via the management tool to have the solution history.

Propose and implement
the solution.

After performing the analysis on all the important points that the product has, Altimea proposes an appropriate solution to your needs without neglecting your business objectives. The solution at technical and functional level will be explained point by point.

The implementation will be carried out by our trained and competent developers under High quality standards and best practices. The customer can view the progress of development in our test environments to check the progression of the final product.

Perform tests
QA (internal and client).

Our QA team will be present throughout the implementation process to understand, from the beginning, How the product is composed. After the implementation, the unit tests, stress, technical, functional and design debt in our environments. The goal is to find and fundamentally correct any incident to ensure the quality of the product. We have QA specialists who assure us the smooth operation, visibility, stability and performance through certification documents.

When the reviews in our environment are successfully completed, the customer can review the product in your test environment for the necessary feedback and close the requirement. This is a process consisting of observation and solution until you have no incident.

Deployment
in production.

Our team of Functional and Usability Analysts perform a survey of requirements and customer needs. At this stage nothing escapes us, we point everything, we collect each feedback that can provide us and serve to give a more personalized approach to the solution we will propose.
